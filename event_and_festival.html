Experience the Vibrancy of Sri Lanka

A journey through culture, color, and celebration. Explore the upcoming festivals and events across the island.

Vesak Poya Festival

May 12, 2026

Country-wide, especially Colombo

The most significant Buddhist festival, celebrating the birth, enlightenment, and passing of Lord Buddha. The entire country is adorned with colorful lanterns, decorations, and 'dansals' (free food stalls).

Kandy Esala Perahera

July - August, 2026

Kandy

One of Asia's most spectacular processions. The "Festival of the Tooth" features thousands of performers, dancers, drummers, and decorated elephants parading the streets of Kandy to honor the Sacred Tooth Relic of the Buddha.

Sinhala & Tamil New Year

April 14, 2026

Country-wide

A major cultural festival that marks the end of the harvest season. Families come together for traditional rituals, feasts, and games, celebrating a new beginning with immense joy and hospitality.

Poson Poya Festival

June 10, 2026

Mihintale & Anuradhapura

Commemorates the introduction of Buddhism to Sri Lanka. Pilgrims flock to Mihintale, the historical site where Buddhism was first preached, for a night of religious ceremonies and devotion.
